Our Story

Our story has begun in the early 1984 in Amman; the capital of Jordan, particularly in Al-Baider area. Where our father Alhajj Ahmad Abu Jbara began creating and inventing the most delicious ingredients and recipes for our own.

As we today serve you our family legacy and lovely meals in all our branches at a high level of accuracy.

Our insistence on arriving at the top is the beginning of continuous hard work, giving us superiority in both local and global markets. we have expanded and released some new branches in different regions in the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan all the way to the neighboring countries, to fulfill our clients' wishes and to achieve our ambitions in reaching as many foreign branches as possible inside the Arab world and the Middle East.

In our group, we make sure to adhere to quality and the highest standards of accuracy and attention in choosing the main ingredients to prepare food, hygiene, and integrated health in our kitchens. From Amman to everywhere we will remain the ancient origin and our story will be told by future generations to continue our journey.

Our ambition is to reach all world countries and take our oriental flavors to the top. Tariq Abu Jabara group is entitled to distinction, creativity, and access to the hearts of all classes of society in all its spectrum and to serve them.
